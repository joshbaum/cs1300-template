Goals

I began this assignment by thinking about possible sites that mesh well with sorting, filtering, and adding items to an aggregator. A grocery store seemed like a logical choice, with each grocery item belonging to a food group, being of a certain size, and having a corresponding price.


My website's goal is to create the streamlined experience consumers expect when they shop for products online. My first step in creating this website was to think about its layout and usability principles.


Usability Principles

Key Thoughts:
  • Clearly display sorting and filtering options at top of page
  • Cohesively display the product name, descriptive attributes, along with its picture
  • Utilize dual column approach to show both food catalog and current cart simultaneously
  • Products as they appear in the cart should be in same format as in the catalog
  • Buttons should be clear and intuitive to use
